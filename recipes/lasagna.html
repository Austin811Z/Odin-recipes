Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna is like building a tasty, layered casserole. You essentially make two main things:

a meaty tomato sauce and a creamy cheese mixture, then stack them up with pasta sheets and bake it until it's bubbly and golden.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.

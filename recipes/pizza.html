Pizza

Description

Making a pizza involves a series of steps from preparing the base ingredients to baking and serving the final product.

This recipe shows how to make campfire pizza when out camping.

Ingredients

  • one pound refrigerated pizza dough
  • quarter cup pizza sauce
  • half cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • half cup sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Place a pizza stone on an outdoor grill directly over a wood fire. You may first need to spread out the wood if the flames are too high.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a flat surface to desired thickness. Place on the pizza stone; cook until golden on one side, about 10 minutes.
  3. Transfer dough to a flat work surface; spread pizza sauce in an even layer onto cooked side of dough, leaving Â½-inch edge bare. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top sauce; top with pepperoni.
  4. Place pizza on the pizza stone; tent with aluminum foil and cook until cheese has melted, about 10 minutes. Transfer pizza to a cutting board. Cool slightly before cutting and serving.
